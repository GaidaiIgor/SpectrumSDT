BVScale

Multiply the BV entries by a scalar value.

Synopsis

#include "slepcbv.h" 
PetscErrorCode BVScale(BV bv,PetscScalar alpha)
Logically Collective on bv

Input Parameters

bv  - basis vectors
alpha  - scaling factor

Note

All active columns (except the leading ones) are scaled.
